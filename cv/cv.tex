\section*{Research Interests}
My research centers on \textbf{Embodied Intelligence}, particularly task planning for
digital humans in household scenarios and for multi-robot systems on industrial
production lines. I also have a background in \textbf{Computer Graphics}, with a focus
on material transfer from photographs to 3D models.
