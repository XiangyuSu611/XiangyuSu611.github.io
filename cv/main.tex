\cvsection{Research Interests}

My research focuses on \textbf{Embodied Intelligence}, particularly task planning for digital humans in household scenarios and for multi-robot systems on industrial production lines. I also work on \textbf{Computer Graphics}, with emphasis on material transfer from photographs to 3D models.

\begin{itemize}

\divider

\cvevent{\textbf{Human Activity Program Generation with Correction-by-Summarization}}{\footnotesize{\textcolor[rgb]{0.4,0.4,0.4}{\textbf{Xiangyu Su}, Jintian Lin, Juntao Jian, Hao Wang, Kai Xu, Ruizhen Hu}} \\ Preprint}{2025}{}
\begin{itemize}
\item Summarizes error-correction experiences into executable code rules to fix non-executable instructions before execution.

\divider

\cvevent{\textbf{MTScan: Material Transfer from Partial Scans to CAD Models}}{\footnotesize{\textcolor[rgb]{0.4,0.4,0.4}{\textbf{Xiangyu Su}, Sida Peng, Oliver van Kaick, Hui Huang, Ruizhen Hu}} \\ CVM 2025}{2025}{}
\begin{itemize}
\item Transfers material information from a partial real-world scan to a CAD model via dense correspondence, combining a material decomposition network with geometry correspondence.
\end{itemize}

\divider

\cvevent{\textbf{Photo-to-Shape Material Transfer for Diverse Structures}}{\footnotesize{\textcolor[rgb]{0.4,0.4,0.4}{Ruizhen Hu, \textbf{Xiangyu Su}, Xiangkai Chen, Oliver van Kaick, Hui Huang}} \\ ACM TOG (SIGGRAPH 2022)}{2022}{}
\begin{itemize}
\item Image translation + material prediction networks for transferring photorealistic, relightable materials from a single photo exemplar to 3D shapes with diverse structures.
\end{itemize}
